About Me

Growing up in Poulsbo, Washington, I attended North Kitsap High School and graduated with honors in 2012. In high school I took multiple engineering focused classes which helped grow my interest in technology.


I am currently a junior at Washington State University working on my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and I will be graduating in December of 2016. I am an active member in the Voiland College of Engineering and Architecture working as an assistant for a smart grid research project, a web developer for The Daily Evergreen, and last semester I was the Secretary for the Computer Security Group.

Programming

In the classroom most of my work has been completed in C, C++, C# and Python. Outside of class I have taught myself HTML, CSS, Javascript, Ruby and Objective C. I have been able to apply this knowledge by working with a volunteer web development group and with other various university related jobs.
